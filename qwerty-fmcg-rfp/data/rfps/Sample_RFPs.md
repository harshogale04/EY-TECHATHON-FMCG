# SAMPLE RFP #1: National Highway Authority

**REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)**

**Date Issued:** November 15, 2024
**Issued By:** National Highway Authority of India (NHAI)
**Project Name:** Four Laning of NH-44 (Bengaluru to Chennai)
**RFP Ref No:** NHAI/2024/12345
**Submission Deadline:** December 30, 2024

---

## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

The National Highway Authority invites sealed bids from eligible Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM) for supply of High Voltage Cables for the Four Laning project of NH-44 highway.

The project spans 250 km and requires comprehensive wiring and cable infrastructure for:
- Toll collection booths (15 locations)
- Electronic weighment stations (8 locations)
- Traffic management centers (3 locations)
- Emergency communication systems

---

## 2. SCOPE OF SUPPLY

### Cable Requirements:

**Item 1: High Voltage Power Cables (1.1kV)**
- Quantity: 1000 meters
- Conductor Size: 240 mm²
- Material: Copper
- Insulation Type: XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene)
- Core Count: 4 cores
- Armoring: Steel tape
- Rated Voltage: 1.1 kV
- Temperature Rating: 90°C

**Item 2: Medium Voltage Cables (0.6kV)**
- Quantity: 500 meters
- Conductor Size: 185 mm²
- Material: Copper
- Insulation Type: XLPE
- Core Count: 3 cores
- Armoring: Steel wire
- Rated Voltage: 0.6 kV
- Temperature Rating: 90°C

**Item 3: Low Voltage Distribution Cables (0.4kV)**
- Quantity: 2000 meters
- Conductor Size: 50 mm²
- Material: Copper
- Insulation Type: PVC
- Core Count: 2 cores
- Armoring: None
- Rated Voltage: 0.4 kV
- Temperature Rating: 70°C

---

## 3. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

All cables must comply with:
- Indian Standards: IS 1554 (Part 1), IS 1554 (Part 2)
- IEC Standards: IEC 60228, IEC 60502-1
- Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) certification mandatory

### Cable Testing Requirements:
1. High Voltage Withstand Test: 3.5 kV for 1 minute
2. Insulation Resistance: Min 10 MΩ/km at 20°C
3. Tensile Strength of Conductor: Min 360 N/mm²
4. Elongation at Break: Min 20%
5. Volume Resistivity of Insulation: Max 10¹⁴ Ω·cm

---

## 4. ACCEPTANCE & TEST REQUIREMENTS

### Pre-Delivery Testing (at Manufacturer's Facility):
- Routine tests on 100% of production
- Type tests on certified samples
- Material certificates for all components

### On-Site Acceptance Testing:
- High Voltage Withstand Test: ₹25,000 per test
- Insulation Resistance Measurement: ₹12,000
- Tensile Strength Verification: ₹18,000
- Mechanical Installation Inspection: ₹15,000
- Documentation & Certification: ₹10,000

**Total Test Costs (Estimated): ₹80,000**

---

## 5. DELIVERY TIMELINE

- **Order Placement:** January 5, 2025
- **Manufacturing Lead Time:** 45 days
- **Delivery on Site:** February 20, 2025
- **Installation & Testing:** March 10, 2025

---

## 6. PRICING

Bidders must provide:
1. **Unit Price (per meter)** for each cable type
2. **Total Material Cost** = Unit Price × Quantity
3. **Test Cost** = As per rates listed in Section 4
4. **Installation Support:** Optional quote
5. **Warranty:** Standard 2-year manufacturing defect warranty

---

## 7. EVALUATION CRITERIA

1. **Technical Compliance:** Must meet 100% of technical specs (mandatory)
2. **Delivery Capability:** Demonstrated track record
3. **Price Competitiveness:** 40% weightage
4. **Certification & Quality:** BIS certification mandatory
5. **After-Sales Support:** 5-year availability of spare parts

---

## 8. SUBMISSION FORMAT

Sealed bid containing:
1. Technical proposal (compliance matrix)
2. Commercial proposal (pricing schedule)
3. Product datasheets (with test certificates)
4. Company credentials & certifications
5. Bank guarantee (5% of total order value)

---

**End of RFP #1**

# SAMPLE RFP #2: Power Grid Corporation LT Cable Supply

**REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL**

**Date:** November 20, 2024
**Client:** Power Grid Corporation of India Ltd (PGCIL)
**Project:** Distribution Network Upgrade - Region-5
**RFP Ref:** PGCIL/LT/2024/67890
**Deadline:** January 10, 2025

---

## 1. PROJECT BACKGROUND

Power Grid seeks sealed tenders from qualified cable manufacturers for supply of Low Tension (LT) distribution cables for grid modernization across 12 districts.

---

## 2. SCOPE OF SUPPLY

**Cable Type 1: 3.5 Core LT Cables (0.4kV)**
- Quantity: 500 meters
- Conductor Size: 50 mm²
- Material: Aluminum (Cu alternative acceptable)
- Insulation: PVC
- Core Count: 3.5 (3 power + 1 earth)
- Temperature Rating: 70°C

**Cable Type 2: 4 Core LT Cables (0.4kV)**
- Quantity: 1000 meters
- Conductor Size: 16 mm²
- Material: Copper
- Insulation: PVC
- Core Count: 4
- Temperature Rating: 70°C

---

## 3. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

- IS 1554 compliance
- BIS certification
- Performance warranty: 10 years
- Emergency supply capacity: 25% of order quantity within 15 days

---

## 4. TESTS & COSTS

- Routine Insulation Test: ₹8,000
- Acceptance Testing: ₹12,000
- Type Test (if required): ₹20,000
- Documentation: ₹5,000

**Total: ₹45,000**

---

## 5. DELIVERY & PAYMENT

- Delivery: March 15, 2025
- Payment: Net 30 days after delivery
- Performance guarantee: 2% of contract value

---
